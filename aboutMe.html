About Me, continued

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Payton and I am a computer science student with a passion for technology, programming, and creative technology. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, with a concentration in Creative Technology, at the University of Regina.

My interest in computers began at a young age, and since then I have been fascinated by the limitless possibilities that technology offers. Throughout my academic career, I have gained a solid foundation in C++, JavaScript, and Python and I have also developed skills in Arduino based hardware, game design, and basic animation.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have also worked on various projects outside of the classroom, including teaching girls coding classes at the Saskatchewan Science Centre, being a featured mentor and activity leader for a Girls in STEAM day conference, and participating in GlobalGameJam.

Following graduation this spring, I will continue my career with Industrial Light and Magic as an Associate Production Engineer, where I have been using my technical skills to support the production process. My responsibilities include designing, implementing, and maintaining custom software tools for the production team, troubleshooting technical issues, and collaborating with various departments to ensure a seamless production workflow.

Apart from programming, I enjoy playing video games, cross-stitching, watching movies, and hanging out with my cat Dip. I find having these creative hobbies keeps my mind challenged in productive ways.
